In production, the [Alceris](https://alceris.com/) analytics script is loaded to measure site usage in anonymised or pseudonymised form. Technical data may be transmitted to the provider. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR or, where required, your consent. See the provider’s privacy policy for details.

## Local storage (theme)

The site stores your display preference (light/dark mode) in the browser (localStorage). This stays on your device and is not sent to a server of this site.
