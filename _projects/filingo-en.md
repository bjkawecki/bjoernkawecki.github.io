Filingo is a **digital Russian vocabulary trainer**. At its centre is spaced repetition: vocabulary is practised with cards, and reviews follow progress so far. Alongside that sits a dictionary with forms, translations, and audio, so practice and look-up share the same material.

The idea grew from my own experience as a student. Suitable material for Russian was scattered across many sources and tedious to gather. What was missing was a coherent system and a path toward completeness. That is what Filingo aims for: preparing learning material systematically and as completely as possible.

The first version was called Bajkal. Conception began in 2021, and it went online in 2024. In 2025 I discontinued Bajkal because I decided on a reconception and fundamental overhaul under the name Filingo. Filingo has been in production since 2025 and is under active development.

Anyone who wants to read more about my thinking on Filingo and learning software can visit the project blog. To try Filingo, use the app site.
